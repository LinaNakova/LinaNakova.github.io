About Me

Hello! My name is Lina and I come from Stip, Macedonia. I'm a first year student at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering. I'm an organized person that pays attention to detail and creatively solves upcoming problems. I've always been best friends with my computer and that's how I decided on what I'm going to study. Even though it is considered to be one of the universities with the toughest programs, I took it on and I'm always focused on what has to be done hoping that I succeed.

Education

I finished elementary school and high school in my home town Stip with a 5.00 gpa and numerous competition prizes in maths, english, chemistry, biology and art. Although studying computer science wasn't my first choice, I enrolled, started studying and fell in love with it immediately. I like some of the courses more than the others, but I like a good challage.
